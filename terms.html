2. Accuracy disclaimer

Product identification is best-effort. AI can make mistakes — especially on minimalist packaging, generic products, partially-visible labels, or new product lines. Always verify the product on the retailer's page before buying.

The Amazon UK price shown is verified at the time you search via SerpAPI's Amazon engine, but prices can change before you complete the purchase. Always re-check on Amazon's own page.

Cost-per-unit calculations and tier classifications (Premium / Mid-tier / Budget) shown on the disambiguation screen are estimates derived from Haiku's training data on UK retail pricing. They are intended as a guide, not as a precise live comparison. Always verify on the retailer's page.

Prices for non-Amazon retailers are sourced from Google Shopping at the time you search and may differ from the retailer's current site price.
