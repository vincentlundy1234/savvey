Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026
Savvey is a free price-checking app. We take your privacy seriously.
What we collect
Email address — only if you set up a price alert. Never sold or shared.
Search queries — stored anonymously. Not linked to you personally.
Location — silently captured if permission granted. Never stored on our servers.
Camera — barcode scanning only, on-device. Images never uploaded.
What we don't collect
No name, no payment details, no browsing history. No ad trackers. No data sold to anyone.
Third-party services
Serper API — powers price search. See serper.dev/privacy.
Supabase — stores price alerts in EU data centres.
