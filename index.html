How Savvey identifies products

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to identify the product you snap, scan, paste, or type. AI can make mistakes - especially on minimalist packaging or new product lines.

Always confirm the product on the retailer's page before buying. Your queries are not used to train the AI model. Photos are processed in real time and not stored.

Verified Amazon UK prices are fetched live via SerpAPI. Other retailer links open the retailer's own search page.
