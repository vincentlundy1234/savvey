Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 May 2026

Savvey is a UK price-comparison Progressive Web App. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

What we collect

When you use Savvey, we collect:

  • Product searches you make (text typed, photos snapped, barcodes scanned, URLs pasted) - to identify the product and return price information.
  • Anonymised usage analytics via PostHog - page views, button taps, search results, app version. We do NOT collect any data that identifies you personally.
  • Local browser storage - recent searches and your preferences (e.g. swipe hint dismissed). This stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

What we do not collect

  • We do NOT collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers.
  • We do NOT collect financial information, payment details, or purchase history.
  • We do NOT track your activity outside of Savvey.
  • We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing.

How we use your data

  • Product searches are sent to Anthropic Claude (AI provider) to identify the product. Anthropic processes these on our behalf under their Data Processing Agreement and does not retain the data for model training. See anthropic.com/privacy for Anthropic's policy.
  • Photos snapped via the camera are sent to Anthropic for product identification, processed in real-time, and are not stored on our servers.
  • Verified Amazon UK prices are fetched via SerpAPI on your behalf. SerpAPI does not retain queries beyond their own logging requirements.
  • Anonymised analytics help us understand which features work and which need improvement.

Where your data is processed

  • Anthropic Claude: US (under EU-US Data Privacy Framework)
  • SerpAPI: US
  • Vercel (hosting): US/EU
  • PostHog (analytics): EU (Frankfurt)

Your rights under UK GDPR

As a UK user, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • File a complaint with the ICO (Information Commissioner's Office)

Because we don't collect personally identifying information, we typically have no data tied to you specifically. If you want to clear your local browser storage, append ?fresh=1 to the Savvey URL or use your browser's site data controls.

Affiliate links

Savvey earns a small commission when you buy via the Amazon UK links shown in the app. This never changes the price you pay. We disclose this clearly on every result screen.

AI use

Savvey uses artificial intelligence (Anthropic Claude) to identify products and synthesise context. AI can make mistakes - always verify product details on the retailer's page before purchasing. No images or queries are used to train the AI model.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be flagged in the app.
